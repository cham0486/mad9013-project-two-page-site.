One-Shot Adventures: What They Are, Why We Love Them, and Recommendations

By Mike Bernier | Original article

Tags: Dungeon Master Tips, One-Shots

What Is a One-Shot?

One-shots are D&D adventures that can be completed in a single 3-4 hour session, offering a contained and fun experience for both players and Dungeon Masters. Whether you’re new to D&D or a seasoned player, one-shots offer the perfect opportunity for quick and exciting gameplay. In this article, we’ll dive deep into what makes a one-shot a great adventure, how to structure one, and why they’ve become a fan favorite in the D&D community.

Why Do We Love One-Shots?

One-shots have quickly become a staple in the D&D community. With time constraints and busy schedules, players and Dungeon Masters alike appreciate the ability to dive into a complete adventure without the need for long-term commitment. One-shots are perfect for one-off sessions, especially when you have a group with varying availability.
